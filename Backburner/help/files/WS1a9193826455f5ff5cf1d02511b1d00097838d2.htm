Using the Favorites
 
 
 

Use the Favorites page to store favorite topics for quick retrieval. While navigating through the Help pages, you can save a page to Favorites by clicking Add to Favorites at the top of the topic.

To show or remove a topic from the Favorites:

  1. In the Favorites, click a link to show that topic.

    It is now the selected favorite.

  2. To remove a favorite, click it and then click Remove selected from Favorites.
